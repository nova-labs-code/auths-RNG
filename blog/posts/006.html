a world without bots

our stats say that we get tons of traffic. from 200 - 400 unique visitors, and almost the same on hits (server requests). when we used another service to see our stats, the stats went from inflated to very very minimal and small.

auth's RNG didn't have a robots.txt file. robots.txt basically judges bots on whenever they can go. obviously, not all bots as good, and they will still crawl the site, but it helps in getting the most accurate stats. so, as of writing this post, i have made a robots.txt file. it basically allows all good bots (archive bots and scrapers, and search engine indexing bots) and gate-keeps everything else. actually, you can see it over here.

and now, we proudly welcome you to a world without bots.

this helps the site by consuming less bandwidth (site assets downloaded size in total every month) and helping the site be online more and be less likely to shutdown

we've also tuned uptimerobot (the service that checks if txt or auth's RNG is down) to instead check every 12 hours, not every 5 minutes. we believe this accounts for a HUGE chunk of tons of our stats.

welcome to a world without bots
