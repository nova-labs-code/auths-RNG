redesigning the game

auth's RNG kept the same style. a bit cluttered, boxy, and a little messy. we heard from yall, and we're making a redesign

introducing the roll area

we compressed roll stuff into one section for your convenience. you might hate it at first, but after some adjusting and getting used to, it becomes really nice. the roll button will be in the roll area, and if you're away from page 1, the old roll button will fade back in so you can auto roll anywhere. also, next to the roll button will have a cool little thing where you can see your last rolls in a nice way!

making things more faster

auth's RNG feels slow and clunky. not on the redesign though! things will load in a lot faster, animations will be snappy, and things wont feel slow. we're also making this the opportunity to make things feel faster, we're not just changing the look.

still keeping the interface

the redesign will feel auth's RNG in it's own way, it's not a full redesign to the point it doesn't feel like auth's RNG. the game will still be incredibly customizable, and again, we're making this the opportunity to make customizing be a much better experience.

nerdy stuff for your use case

we are also FULLY changing the purpose of dev mode. it used to just be a random little toggle where you have a button to see random stats but now we're fully changing what it is with VIM style commands and much more if you're feeling nerdy today!

changing the music

music ain't free anymore, and we used some copyrighted tracks. unfortunately some copyright holders have been going against us, and we're proudly releasing the auth's RNG OST!

you can test the redesign on the nightly version.
