bing block

neocities, the web host that hosts this site, has written on their blog that neocities sites are not indexed by Bing due to Bing blocking neocities.

this severely hurts our chances of getting auth's RNG ranked and seen by tons of other new people, and hurts our chances of growing this community.

a lot of search engines and browsers use Bing for searching and indexing, and because Bing has blocked neocities sites (including auth's RNG) we're starting to lose traction. we can only get ranked and seen on Google, although many people are starting to ditch Google and use DuckDuckGo which uses Bing, and other search engines that exclusively use Bing.

this is why when you search "auth's RNG" on Bing, it only shows the itch.io page and many other sites relating to it. but if you search "auth's RNG" on Google, it shows this website as the first result.

why post this if it doesnt affect me?

it affects us. it affects the community. it affects everything. this means that our community will be only be seen by word of mouth, and it takes a long time to get a site, or a game, have people talking about the game. this also demolishes our stats, as most of our visitors are search engine crawlers and other bots that help our site become a bit more seen (and also just ruining our site). very little of our visitors are actually humans, and we want to make it be more humans instead of just having inflating stats, but we can't go any further.

yes, we know that Bing only owns about 4% of market share in search engines while Google has 90%, but still, Bing is still the second largest search engine, and that's a fact.

if you want more info, read neocities's blog post about the bing block
