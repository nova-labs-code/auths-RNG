who we are
auth's RNG is a free browser-based game operated by auth (the developer), an individual based in canada. this privacy policy explains how data is handled when you play at authsrng.xyz and any subdomains (nightly.authsrng.xyz, backup.authsrng.xyz, leaderboard.authsrng.xyz).
tl;dr — we collect almost nothing. nearly all your game data lives in your own browser. no account required. no email. no tracking pixels. no ads.
what data we collect
data stored only in your browser (we never see this):
  • your rarity inventory, roll count, achievements, shop upgrades, potions, anomalies, gauntlet progress, wishing well history, daily/weekly streak info, and notification history — all saved to your browser's localStorage
  • your chosen game settings (theme, music preference, etc.)
  • a randomly generated uuid that identifies your save slot locally
data sent to our servers (only if you opt in):
  • cloud backups — your encoded save data, identified by your random uuid. you must explicitly enable this in settings.
  • leaderboard — total rolls, total rarities, rarest rarity (name & denominator), total playtime in seconds, and total points. you must explicitly opt in and choose a public username.
  • concurrent user ping — a session id (random string, not tied to you) is sent once per minute to count how many people are online. it is not logged, not stored, and not associated with any personal data.
data collected automatically (server logs):
  • standard web server access logs may temporarily capture ip addresses and user-agent strings as part of normal http operations. these are not processed for profiling, are not sold or shared, and are rotated regularly.
we do not use analytics platforms (google analytics, mixpanel, etc.), advertising networks, fingerprinting scripts, or any third-party tracking. external services loaded by the game are listed in the third-party services section below.
how we use your data
we use data only to provide the game's features:
  • cloud backup data is used solely to restore your save when you request it
  • leaderboard data is displayed publicly on the leaderboard page, as you agreed when opting in
  • server logs are used only for diagnosing technical issues and maintaining uptime
we do not use your data for advertising, profiling, marketing, analytics, or selling to third parties. ever.
where data is stored
there are two distinct hosting layers:
  • game files (static frontend) — authsrng.xyz is hosted on cloudflare pages, a global edge network. the game's html, js, and assets are served from cloudflare's infrastructure worldwide, not from a single server in canada.
  • backend data (cloud backups & leaderboard) — stored on servers operated by the developer, physically located in canada, and accessed via a cloudflare tunnel (no port forwarding).
because the frontend is served via cloudflare pages and all backend traffic routes through a cloudflare tunnel, cloudflare's infrastructure processes every request made to the game — including ip addresses and request headers — subject to cloudflare's privacy policy. cloudflare acts as a data processor on our behalf and is not permitted to use your data for its own purposes.
your local game data never leaves your device unless you explicitly enable cloud backups or join the leaderboard.
do we share your data?
no. we do not sell, rent, trade, or share your data with any third parties — advertising companies, data brokers, or anyone else. the only "sharing" that occurs is:
  • leaderboard stats are displayed publicly on the leaderboard page (only if you opted in)
  • if we were ever compelled by canadian law (e.g. a valid court order), we would comply — but we hold so little identifying data that there is almost nothing to share
cloud backups
cloud backups are entirely opt-in. when enabled:
  • your save data is encoded and sent to our backup server, identified only by a randomly generated uuid stored in your browser
  • no account, email, name, or personal information is collected
  • you can delete your cloud backup at any time from the settings page
  • disabling cloud backups does not delete existing cloud data — you must use the "delete backup" button to remove it from our server
  • backups are provided as-is with no uptime guarantee. we recommend also keeping local save exports as a backup
your backup key is the uuid in your browser's localStorage under cloudBackupUid. if you clear your localStorage without saving this key, you will lose access to your cloud backup. we cannot recover it for you.
leaderboard
the leaderboard is entirely opt-in. when you join:
  • you pick a permanent public username (3–20 characters)
  • your game stats are submitted automatically every 15 minutes while you play
  • submitted stats are publicly visible to anyone who visits the leaderboard page
  • your username cannot be changed after joining
  • you can opt out at any time — this permanently deletes your entry from our server
the same random uuid is used for both cloud backups and leaderboard identity — you have one identity across both systems, and it is not linked to any personal information.
cookies & local storage
auth's RNG does not use cookies for tracking or advertising. game data is stored in your browser's localStorage (not cookies). localStorage data stays on your device and is not sent to any server unless you opt into cloud backups.
you can clear your localStorage at any time through your browser settings. doing so will permanently delete your local save data. if you have cloud backups enabled, you can restore from there afterward.
third-party services
the following third-party scripts or services are loaded when you visit the game. we do not control their data practices — please review their own privacy policies:
  • cloudflare — serves the game frontend via cloudflare pages (global edge CDN) and proxies all backend api traffic via cloudflare tunnel. sees all request traffic (ip, headers) for both. cloudflare privacy policy
  • instant.page — preloading library served via cdnjs. may record requests to cdnjs infrastructure. instant.page
  • librecounter — open-source hit counter. records a page view when you visit. librecounter.org
  • hitscounter.dev — visit counter. records a page view when you visit. hitscounter.dev
none of these services are used for advertising or behavioural tracking. they are open-source or privacy-oriented tools, but their own policies govern what they collect.
data retention
we retain data only as long as necessary:
  • cloud backups — kept indefinitely until you delete them via the in-game settings, or until you contact us requesting removal
  • leaderboard entries — kept until you opt out in-game, at which point they are permanently deleted from our server
  • server access logs — rotated and deleted on a rolling basis (typically within 30 days). not processed for profiling.
  • concurrent user session ids — not stored at all. they exist only in memory for the duration of the request.
local browser data (localStorage) is entirely under your control and persists until you clear your browser data or use the in-game wipe feature.
breach notification
in the event of a breach of security safeguards involving your personal information that poses a real risk of significant harm, we will:
  • notify affected individuals as soon as feasible
  • report the breach to the office of the privacy commissioner of canada (OPC) as required under pipeda
  • maintain a record of the breach as required by law
given that we hold almost no identifying personal information, the practical scope of any breach would be limited — your game data is not linked to your name, email, or any account. the highest-risk scenario would be exposure of leaderboard usernames paired with game statistics, which are already public.
your rights (pipeda / alberta pipa)
under canada's personal information protection and electronic documents act (pipeda) and alberta's personal information protection act (pipa), you have the right to:
  • access — request what personal information we hold about you
  • correction — request correction of inaccurate personal information
  • withdrawal of consent — opt out of data collection at any time (for cloud backups and leaderboard, use the in-game settings)
  • deletion — request deletion of any server-side data we hold about you
  • complaint — file a complaint with the office of the privacy commissioner of canada (OPC) at priv.gc.ca, or the office of the information and privacy commissioner of alberta at oipc.ab.ca
because we do not collect identifying personal information (no name, email, or account), most of these rights apply primarily to cloud backup and leaderboard data, which you can manage entirely in-game. to make a formal request, contact us at the address below.
children & minors
auth's RNG is a general-audience browser game. we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. if you believe a child has submitted personal information to our services, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.
because we don't require accounts or personal info, children are not identifiable in our systems unless a parent/guardian specifically contacts us about a cloud backup uuid.
